About Me

Front-End Developer

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I’m Gokulmani, a passionate Front-End Developer based in Salem, currently in the final year of my B.E. in Electronics and Communication Engineering. My journey into front-end development began with a fascination for technology and its potential to create engaging, user-friendly experiences. I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various front-end frameworks. In my projects, I focus on delivering visually stunning and highly functional websites. Each project has been a learning experience, pushing me to explore new technologies and innovative solutions. What sets me apart is my commitment to continuous learning. I am always seeking new ways to expand my horizons and fuel my creativity. I invite you to explore my portfolio and see the passion and dedication I bring to my work.
